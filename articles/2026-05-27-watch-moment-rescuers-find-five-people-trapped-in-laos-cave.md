Rescuers in Laos have found five villagers alive inside a flooded cave after they were trapped for a week following heavy rain and landslides.Two people are still missing, rescue teams said.Footage shared by the rescuers showed cave divers crawling through narrow, muddy passageways.The seven people were part of a group of villagers who had gone into the cave in search of gold deposits and wildlife, but could not get out as the cave's entrance was blocked.Read more on this story here.
