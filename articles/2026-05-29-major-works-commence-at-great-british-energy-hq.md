The Government Property Agency's fit-out works have begun at the permanent home of Great British Energy.

  










          
    

        THE Government Property Agency (GPA) has reached an important milestone as it progresses plans to establish an office space for a new, publicly-owned energy company.

On Monday, the GPA-led fit-out works began at the Marischal Square development in Aberdeen, Scotland, which will serve as the headquarters of Great British Energy (GBE). 

The project will ensure GBE’s workforce are provided with a workspace that boosts their productivity, while supporting collaboration and different ways of working.

Read more about this on the GPA’s website.
