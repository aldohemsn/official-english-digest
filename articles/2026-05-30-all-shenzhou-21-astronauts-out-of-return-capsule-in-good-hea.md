JIUQUAN, May 29 -- The three astronauts of the Shenzhou-21 crewed mission have all left the return capsule of the Shenzhou-22 spaceship, and they are in good health condition, setting a new record for the longest stay in space by a Chinese astronaut crew.
The return capsule, carrying astronauts Zhang Lu, Wu Fei and Zhang Hongzhang, touched down at the Dongfeng landing site in north China's Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region on Friday.
